A Few Words About Me

Currently, I am a student of Marketing and Communcation, but also experienced in photography, videography, and journalism. I have been filming, editing, and writting for many years, and now my skills developed to become a powerful force in the world of Public Relations.

Where it all started

I started my filming and editing life in creating youtube videos. Of ourse, I tried to hop on the gaming trends, but alas, I discovered I had a lot to learn before I could market myself like that. That's when I started pursuing Photography and Videography, and I started creating projects for clientele. Check out my Google Scholar page!

Skill development

After creating a business, I found a lot of room for improvement, so I started doing work for the Air National Guard's Public Affairs section. In 2020 I created a journalism video that was broadcasted internationally, followed by writing an article that was published mid 2023. The volunteering would eventually become a career change and in April 2024 I will be transitioning to doing work for them part time, and training under them in Photography, Videography, and journalism.

Where I wish to go

As of right now my current career is with the VA, working in developing programs to help veterans who are isolated and suicidal. A lot of these require marketing and project management, as I have to reach the veterans and engage with them, as well as develop the programs as time goes on. I've learned that project management is a passion of mine in that process, and now I use all my skills I've gathered over my life to effectively communicate, market, and curate events, programs, and resources for veterans to improve their outlook on life.
